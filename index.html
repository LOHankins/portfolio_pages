  • Wine Up, Wind Down

    My third GA project was a group effort with Leland Jordan and Habby Whyte. We created an app to match food with the proper wines. The work was divided into HTML/CSS, Angular and the API. My assignment was the API which including defining, creating and seeding the database. And writing the code to respond to data requests and deliver the request in JSON format. My intention is to continue work on this app to further my understanding of and to increase my skill level in Angular.
